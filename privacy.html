Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bets and Guesses (referred to as "the Game", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile application and use our services.

By playing the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection

We believe in data minimization. We only collect the minimal amount of information required to provide you with a functional multiplayer experience:

  • User-provided Names: To join or host a game lobby, you choose a player nickname. This name is temporarily stored in our database so other players in the lobby can identify you.
  • Game State Data: Game choices, guesses, and score tallies are processed and stored temporarily in our database in order to synchronize the game round across all participating devices.
  • Device Information: We do not track your device's unique hardware identifier, location, or contacts. The Game only uses network permissions to connect to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party infrastructure providers to run our backend multiplayer services:

  • Supabase: Our backend database and real-time multiplayer synchronization. Supabase acts as a data processor. For more details, please review the Supabase Privacy Policy.

We do not share, sell, or rent your nickname or game data to any advertisers, marketing networks, or other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Deletion

All game lobbies, player rosters, and in-game scoring states are transient. Data associated with closed game lobbies is periodically cleared or overwritten. We do not store persistent player profiles or track you across sessions.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Game is suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children. Since we do not request emails, real names, phone numbers, or persistent identifiers, the Game complies with children's privacy protection standards.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
